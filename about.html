    Hi, my name is Rithu.

    My mission in life is to not let it be boring. Making the most of every moment is what I strive for, whether that’s studying a hundred disciplines, traveling the world, or helping others. I am now studying Marketing and Global Analysis at the Wharton School of Business (UPenn) with a side pursuit in Fine Arts.

    I combined my fascination for art with a will to help others by creating Allo, a social enterprise dedicated to feeding poverty-ridden children. Through Allo, I’ve sold over 1000 stickers online, in galleries, and at events. Over the years I’ve also exhibited regionally and nationally, been awarded several National Scholastic Gold and Silver Keys, and have my work displayed in the state legislature’s permanent collection.

    I hope you enjoy viewing my art as much as I enjoyed making them.
